## Project Overview

Team-based data visualization internship project developing an **interactive business intelligence dashboard** for Excelerate's global learning programs. Transformed fragmented educational data into actionable insights supporting **52.1K learners** across **146 countries** through comprehensive ETL processes and strategic analytics.

**Live Dashboard:** [View Interactive Dashboard](https://lookerstudio.google.com/reporting/2f96741e-e9c4-4b02-8b94-6f8547626f7a)

## Major Findings

### Geographic Distribution
- **Global Reach:** Strong presence across 146 countries
- **Regional Focus:** High engagement in developing regions
- **Expansion Opportunities:** South America, Eastern Europe, Central Asia

### Campaign Effectiveness
- **Top Performer:** "Jan. Internship: Virtual Internship" (27.5K clicks)
- **Drop-off Issue:** 38.3K clicks → 16.1K views (47% loss)
- **Cost Efficiency:** Identified high-performing, cost-effective campaigns

### Temporal Trends
- **Peak Performance:** 2023 showed highest engagement (79.2M cohort size)
- **Recent Challenge:** Decline to 23.8M in 2025 requiring intervention

---

## Strategic Recommendations

### Immediate Actions
1. **Optimize Landing Pages** - Address 47% click-to-view drop-off rates
2. **Campaign Timing** - Align with academic calendars for better engagement
3. **Regional Focus** - Target underserved markets for expansion

### Long-term Initiatives
4. **Performance Benchmarking** - Establish KPIs for continuous improvement
5. **Predictive Analytics** - Implement forecasting for trend anticipation
6. **Personalization Engine** - Develop targeted learner experiences
